Safe, Decentralized and Democratized AI built w/ LonScript, the Decentralized-Internet SDK, Mojo, and the LLM Standard

The Library

The AI competition is heating up, and with a bunch of new LLMs hitting the market, a lot of them rely on inefficient computing and are basically just search engine reindexing algorithms. That’s where OpenPeer AI steps in. It’s decentralized and built on the decentralized-internet SDK, using BOINC as its foundation. By utilizing Mojo, LonScript, OpenPeer, and LLM standards, it offers one of the most scalable platforms for AI, ensuring accuracy and predictability.
